Inventory & Reporting

01Introduction

Coorg Water Park ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our employees, contractors, and authorized users who use our internal enterprise mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it secure.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. This App is intended exclusively for internal business use and is not available to the general public.

02Information We Collect

2.1 Camera & QR / Barcode Data

The App requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes as part of internal operations (e.g., ticketing, inventory, access control). The camera feed is processed in real time and is not recorded or stored on your device.

2.2 Scanned Data Uploaded to Our Server

The data extracted from scanned QR codes or barcodes (e.g., ticket IDs, product codes, access tokens) is transmitted to and stored on Coorg Water Park's internal servers. This data may include:

  • Scan timestamp and location within the park
  • Barcode or QR code value
  • Device identifier (for audit purposes)
  • User ID or employee / staff account associated with the scan

2.3 Device & Usage Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information to support app performance and security, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Error logs and crash reports

03How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for internal business operations, including:

  • Validating tickets and managing park entry / access control
  • Inventory tracking and operational reporting
  • Auditing and security monitoring
  • Troubleshooting technical issues with the App
  • Ensuring compliance with internal policies

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to Coorg Water Park's internal operations.

04Data Storage & Security

All data uploaded from the App is stored on secure servers maintained by or on behalf of Coorg Water Park. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS / TLS)
  • Access controls limiting data visibility to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews of our systems

Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the operational purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law.

05Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data may only be disclosed:

  • To authorized internal staff with a legitimate business need
  • To IT service providers or infrastructure partners acting on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations
  • When required by law, regulation, or a valid legal order

06Your Rights

As an authorized user of this internal App, you may:

  • Request access to data collected about your account or device
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational retention requirements

To exercise these rights, please contact your IT Administrator or HR department.

07Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through internal channels (e.g., email or in-app notification). Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
