🌊 Welcome to Coorg Water Park 🌊
Nestled in the lush green hills of Coorg (Kodagu), Karnataka, Coorg Water Park is the region's most exciting water and adventure destination. Whether you're looking for heart-pumping rides, relaxing pools, or a fun-filled family day out — we have it all!
🎢 Thrilling Rides & Attractions
From high-speed water slides and wave pools to lazy rivers and rain dance zones, Coorg Water Park offers a wide range of attractions for every age group. Feel the rush of our signature rides or unwind in our calm leisure pools — the choice is yours!
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Perfect for Everyone
Whether you're visiting with family, friends, or colleagues, Coorg Water Park guarantees an unforgettable experience. Our dedicated kids' zone ensures little ones have a safe and splashing good time, while adults enjoy premium rides and relaxation areas.
🌿 Surrounded by Nature
Set against the breathtaking backdrop of Coorg's misty hills and lush forests, our park offers a unique blend of adventure and natural beauty. Breathe in the fresh air and enjoy world-class water fun in one of India's most scenic locations.
🍽️ Food & Refreshments
Refuel with delicious snacks and meals at our in-park food stalls and restaurants. From local Karnataka delicacies to popular fast food options, there's something to satisfy every craving.
